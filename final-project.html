The Bluetooth-Controlled Arm (BCA) 1.0
2021

I built this arm as a final project for my Digital Fabrication class at Harvard Summer School. This project used HC-05 bluetooth modules along with a a Metro M0 Express microcontroller and ESP-32 Huzzah microcontroller, as well as multiple accelerometers and flex sensors to determine position of the arm and then broadcast that to the bluetooth one.
